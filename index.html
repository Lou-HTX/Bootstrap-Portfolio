About Me

My name is Luis Moreno, and I enjoy creating and tinkering. I have seventeen years of customer service experience. Twelve years of professional troubleshooting and problem solving experience. I am dedicated, outgoing, and a team player. I am a graphic designer with a reprographics and information technology background. In my spare time I work on vintage mopeds. I have a passion for restoring and modifying seventies and eighties model pedal start mopeds. My collection of mopeds have reached up to sixteen at one time. At the moment, the current number has been reduced to six. Other passions include, art, technology, sports, personal finance, and many other “nerdy” hobbies. I am pursuing a career in web development because I believe it will bridge my creative side with my technical background.
